### sage (soil & agricultural guidance engine)

portable soil analysis • ai-powered recommendations • open source precision agriculture

sage started with one simple question.

**why does a farmer have to wait days for a soil test when the technology already exists to make it much faster?**

so we decided to build our own solution.

sage is an open-source precision agriculture project built around **agropen** — a portable soil scanner that helps farmers understand their soil in minutes instead of waiting for lab reports.

the idea is simple.

collect the data.

understand it.

help the farmer make a better decision.

eventually we'd like sage to become a complete farming ecosystem with ai recommendations, dashboards, mobile apps and smart farm automation.


## what can it do?

### current prototype

- reads soil moisture
- reads soil temperature
- measures air temperature & humidity
- measures light intensity
- displays everything on an oled
- transfers data to the sage hub over usb

### future plans

- ph sensing
- ec sensing
- npk analysis
- better ai
- offline llm
- mobile app
- smart farm automation
